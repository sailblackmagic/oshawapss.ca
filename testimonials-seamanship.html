April, 2013:

The Seamanship course provided by the Oshawa Power and Sail Squadron (OPSS) was, in my opinion and experience, delivered in a professional and reliable manner. Mark Ashworth (the instructor)went well beyond the requirements of the course material by providing access to his personal equipment and experiences. I highly recommend the seamanship course to both novice and seasoned boaters. Everyone will gain practical knowledge for the safe and effective methods of boat operation. Thank you Mark, Suzanne, Christine, Brian and all those people at the OPSS that made this quality course possible.

Sincerely, R. White
